# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Residential groups (families, couples, roommates) who need to manage shared finances without corporate bureaucracy. Users range from tech-savvy financial leads to "analog" members who just need their expenses tracked.

## Product Purpose
Divi (Family) is a fintech dashboard that feels like an adventure game. It aims to humanize financial data through expressive mascots, tactile interfaces, and mutual trust-based sharing, making household accounting feel like a collaborative adventure rather than a chore.

## Brand Personality
Playful, Warm, Adventure, Tactile. Like a Pixar storyboard on cream paper. It is expressive and animated but remains grounded in financial utility.

## Anti-references
- Corporate/Enterprise RBAC systems (rigid permissions).
- Cold, flat, purely utilitarian fintech dashboards (e.g., legacy banking apps).
- External drop shadows and "clean" neon aesthetics.
- "Productivity theater" or buzzword-heavy marketing.

## Design Principles
- **Playful Fintech**: Humanize data with mascots and micro-animations.
- **Tactile & Paper-like**: Use warm off-white canvas, noise textures, and inset depths.
- **Focus Mode (Zen)**: Hide unrelated context during complex tasks to reduce cognitive load.
- **Uninterrupted Flow**: Preserve progress during nested sub-tasks.
- **Floating Navigation**: Use decoupled 'Island' navigation to create depth and focus.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **High Contrast**: Maintain ≥4.5:1 contrast (WCAG AA) even on warm-tinted backgrounds.
- **Reduced Motion**: Provide crossfade or instant alternatives for all expressive animations.
- **Inclusion**: Support virtual/orphan members for residents who don't use the app directly.

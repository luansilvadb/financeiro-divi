Core Data Entity: The Transaction

To support "Payer vs. Source" logic, every entry needs to track three entities.

Ledger Entry Schema
Entity 1

Source (Who Paid)

The physical card or cash used (e.g., Luan's Credit Card).

Entity 2

Payer (Responsible)

The person whose debt this is (e.g., House Fund or Luan).

Entity 3

Beneficiaries

Who the money was spent on (e.g., Split 50/50 between Luan and Partner).

Example: "I paid the pizza with my flatmate's card."
Source: Flatmate | Payer: Luan | Beneficiaries: Luan & Flatmate
